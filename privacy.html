Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how user data is handled within the Hairelya app. By using the app, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Data Collection

Hairelya does not collect or transmit personal information. All data is stored locally on the device or, if enabled, in iCloud. No login is required, and no name, email, or contact information is collected.

2. Data Stored

Data is stored only locally or in iCloud (for PRO users only). No data is shared externally. We do not store any personal information such as names or emails.

  • Hair care routine and preferences
  • Timeline photos with notes
  • Used products (name, type, brand)
  • Reminders and schedule settings
  • Hair profile details (type, structure, scalp, goals)

3. iCloud Sync

For PRO users, data is synced with their private iCloud account using Apple's privacy and encryption protections. We have no access to iCloud data.

4. Notifications

The app uses local notifications only. No data is transmitted or stored externally.

5. Advertising

The FREE version may display banner ads from third-party providers (e.g., Google AdMob) using anonymous identifiers. No personalized tracking is performed by the app.

6. In-App Purchases

PRO features can be unlocked via Apple’s in-app purchase system. No payment data is stored by us.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete your data manually from within the app or by uninstalling it. iCloud data must be removed via your Apple settings.

8. Policy Changes

Any important changes to this policy will be communicated via the app.

9. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to email us at: support@hairelya.com
